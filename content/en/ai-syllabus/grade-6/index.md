### **1. Review of Previous Topics**

### **2. Brain and Computers**

- **Human Nervous System (CNS/PNS)**
  - Introduction to how humans collect input through senses, process it, and respond.
- **Procedural Logic vs. AI Programs**
  - Understanding the difference between traditional computer programs and AI programs.

### **3. Applications of AI**

- **Education**
  - The role of AI in enhancing learning experiences.
- **Robots**
  - Exploring how robots use AI to perform tasks.
- **Self-driving Cars**
  - Understanding how AI enables cars to drive themselves.
- **Healthcare**
  - Learning about AI's role in medicine and improving patient care and inclusive access.

### **4. AI for Social Good**

- **Real-time Translation**
  - Learning that AI can instantly translate languages to connect people globally.
- **Healthcare Advancements**
  - Discovering how AI contributes to disease detection, drug discovery, and inclusive healthcare.

### **5. Types of Problems AI Needs to Solve**

- **Classification**
  - Sorting items into different categories.
- **Clustering**
  - Grouping similar data points without predefined categories.
- **Recommendation**
  - Suggesting products or content based on user preferences.
- **Prediction**
  - Using data to forecast future events.
- **Generation**
  - Creating new content like text, images, or music.

### **6. Understanding of Data Types**

- **Text**
  - Understanding that AI can process and understand written words.
- **Images**
  - Learning that AI can analyze pictures by interpreting pixels.
