AI Curriculum

Grade 5

Overview of AI syllabus for Grades 4 to 9.

1. Review of Grade 4 Topics

2. Intelligence Basics

  • Perception
    • Recognizing that humans interpret complex information from vision, hearing, and other senses.
  • Reasoning
    • Understanding that humans use thinking skills to solve problems in daily life.
  • Memory
    • Learning about how humans store and recall information for future use in reasoning and problem-solving.

3. Types of AI Tools

  • Conversational AI
    • Exploring AI chatbots and virtual assistants that can communicate with users.
  • Image Generation
    • Understanding that AI can create new images from data.
  • Audio Generation
    • Discovering that AI can produce sounds or music.
  • Video Generation
    • Learning that AI can create videos using algorithms.
  • Speech Generation
    • Understanding that AI can produce human-like speech and voices.

4. Popular AI Tools

  • DALL-E, Midjourney
    • Creating images using AI technology.
  • Eleven Labs, Murf AI
    • Creating or editing speech using AI technology.
  • Sora, Runway ML
    • Creating video clips using AI technology.

5. Using AI Tools

  • Prompt Refining
    • Learning to write effective prompts.
  • Conversational Iterative Prompting
    • Practicing how to achieve desired results by refining prompts during conversations with AI.

6. Safe and Fair AI

  • Unbiased
    • Ensuring AI treats all users fairly without discrimination.
  • Inclusive
    • Making AI accessible and useful for everyone.
  • Safe (Asimov’s 3 Laws of Robotics)
    • Understanding basic rules designed to keep AI beneficial and safe.

7. AI for Social Good

  • Accessibility
    • Exploring how AI helps people with disabilities overcome challenges.
  • Education for Everyone
    • Understanding ways AI makes learning accessible to all students.
